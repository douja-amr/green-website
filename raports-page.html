Annual Raport of 2019

2019 has been a remarkable year for the African Green movement and the Green African Foundation. It was the year of renewal of hope and civil engagement in Africa ... the year of the Green Wave! Together with our partners across the continent, we worked tirelessly to contribute to the shaping of a political agenda that leads us to a Africa of social and climate justice, solidarity and lived participatory democracy. With African democracy being threatened by rampant corruption, anti-liberal populist movements, serious attacks on the rule of law, as well as climate inaction by many governments, reimagining our African societies through exploring bold ideas for a green future is imperative.
